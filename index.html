Description

Rat Crew Studios is an independent game developer focusing on role-playing games. The company was founded by four childhood friends with a lifelong dream of making video games for a living. The studio’s current project is Broken, an upcoming RPG based on the Praedor graphic novels and tabletop RPG created by Petri Hiltunen and Ville Vuorela.


In Broken, players will assume the role of a Praedor, a title given to adventurers who risk their lives battling deadly beasts in search for treasure in the nightmarish ruins of a destroyed world.


Broken is being developed for PC and consoles on Unreal Engine 4.
