Description

Rat Crew Studios is a game studio from Oulu, Finland.

We have dedicated ourselves to creating fun, innovative and ambitious roleplaying games and transmedia with a Finnish attitude and perseverance.

We focus quality over quantity using contemporary and future technologies.

We are developing games for PC and console platforms.
