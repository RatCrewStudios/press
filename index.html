Description

Rat Crew Studios is an independent game studio from Oulu, Finland. We have dedicated ourselves to creating fun, innovative and ambitious role-playing games and transmedia with a Finnish attitude and perseverance. We focus quality over quantity using contemporary and future technologies.
We are developing games for Windows, PlayStation 4 and Xbox One.
